Regression Analysis

Regression analysis helps us understand relationships between different variables. In this activity, we look at how minutes played per game relate to points scored for WNBA players. The trendline shows the average relationship and can be used to make predictions.

The trendline (calculated using Ordinary Least Squares) shows the general direction of the data. Hover over the data points to see player details, or hover over the trendline to see the regression equation and R-squared value.
