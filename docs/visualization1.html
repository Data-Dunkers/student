Data Visualizations

Welcome to your first Data Dunkers data exploration! In this activity, you’ll work with real NBA player statistics from the 2024–2025 season to understand how different players contribute to the game.

By the end of this activity, you will be able to:

  • Interpret common basketball statistics used in sports analytics
  • Identify differences between high-volume scorers, efficient shooters, playmakers, and rebounders
  • Use data and visuals to compare players and understand their roles
  • Recognize patterns and anomalies across the league
  • Communicate findings using a short, evidence-based data story

Visualizations help you see relationships and trends in the data. This first visualization is a scatterplot that shows how players' field goal percentages relate to the points they scored.

Identify some players that stand out from the rest. Hover over different points to see player names and statistics.

Questions:

  1. Name one player who stands out in this visualization.
  2. Why do you think this player is an outlier?
  3. If you were the coach, what questions would you ask about this player?
